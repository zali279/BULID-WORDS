BUILD WORDS !!

How to play ?

    Instruction :

  1. The player is given 6 random letters, which the player must combine to make a word.
  2. In each level there is a specific task that the player must complete in order to complete the level (#number of words in #spicific time).
  3. Throughout the levels(total of 10), the player's time will decrease and the number of target words will increase.

    Score:

  1. Based on the number of letters in the word, the player collects COINS.
  2. When a player completes a task before half time, he receives a diamond.

    Advance help:

  1. A hint is provided by 5 coins to the player (one word )
  2. TIME++ : By spending one diamond, the player receives an additional 10 seconds
  3. WORD-- : By spending 3 coins , the target word in the level will increase
